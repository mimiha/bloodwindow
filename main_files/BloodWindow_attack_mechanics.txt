Triangle Button:
	-ranged button for felix (throwing knives, bow)
	-slow-strong attack for allen
	-exits out of item/spell screen

Square button:
	-weak-fast attack

X Button:
	-action button, randomizes with O
	-in meat machines, is the jump button
	-selects item/spell in the item/spell screens

O Button:
	-action button, randomizes with X
	-in meat machines, this is the 'pick up/set down' button

R1 Button:
	-turns main 4 buttons into the hotkeyed spells, spell list 1

L1 button:
	-turns main 4 buttons into the hotkeyed spells, spell list 2

R2 button:
	-joint move between allen and felix, (i.e God reel roulette)

L2 button:
	-taunt

Left D-pad:
	-pulls up item list

Right D-pad:
	-pulls up skill/magic list

Up/Down D-pad:
	-scroll through the lists
